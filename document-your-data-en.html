HTR-United

HTR-United is a Github organization without any other form of legal personality. It aims at gathering HTR/OCR models for and transcriptions of all periods and style of writing, mostly but not exclusively in French.
It was born from the mere necessity -for projects- to possess potentiel ground truth to rapidly train models on smaller corpora.

Steps

Follow the examples and note that you can always manually modify the output. This form only helps you structure the description and ensure that every item is documented.
And copy the output !
You can add the description to the catalog in different ways, depending on your knowledge of Github:
  • Fork the htr-united project, modify htr-united.yml and create a Pull Request.
  • Or create an issue in the htr-united project so that someone can modify htr-united.yml for you.
