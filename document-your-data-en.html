HTR-United

HTR-United is a Github organization without any other form of legal personality. It aims at gathering HTR/OCR models for and transcriptions of all periods and style of writing, mostly but not exclusively in French.
It was born from the mere necessity -for projects- to possess potentiel ground truth to rapidly train models on smaller corpora.
